Welcome to Miku

A filesystem-owned personal wiki that lives inside your own folders — plain Markdown, instant search, and a voice of its own.

#guide #meta Updated 2 days ago · 3 min read
Every page you see here is a real .md file on disk. Edit it in Miku or in your favourite editor — both stay perfectly in sync.

What makes Miku different

  • Your files, your rules. No database, no lock-in. Miku reads and writes the Markdown folder you already keep.
  • Wikilinks that just work. Type [[Page]] and Miku resolves it, backlinks and all.
  • Live everywhere. Changes on disk stream into the open page instantly — no refresh, no save dance.

Your first page

Spin one up from the sidebar, or from the terminal — Miku watches the folder either way:

terminal 
$ miku new "My First Note"
→ created notes/my-first-note.md
$ miku serve --watch
→ live at http://localhost:4321
“The best wiki is the one that disappears into your own files.” — that's the whole idea behind Miku.

Ready? Hop into Getting Started or play in the Markdown Sandbox.
